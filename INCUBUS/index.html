Incubus is an American rock band formed in Calabasas, California in 1991. The band has a characteristic sound that combines various musical styles such as alternative rock, funk rock, nu metal and electronic music. Incubus consists of members such as Brandon Boyd (vocals), Mike Einziger (guitar), Jose Pasillas (drums), Chris Kilmore (keyboards) and Ben Kenney (bass guitar).

The band started to gain a local fan base with their independent album "Fungus Amongus" released in 1995. However, they made their real debut in 1999 with the album "Make Yourself". This album included the hit singles "Drive" and "Pardon Me" and was a widespread commercial success. They continued their success with albums such as "Morning View" in 2001 and "A Crow Left of the Murder..." in 2004.

Incubus released many successful albums throughout their career. In 2006, the album "Light Grenades" was released and peaked at number one on the Billboard 200. In 2011, "If Not Now, When?" After releasing their album, "Trust Fall (Side A)", they released the EP in 2015.

While the band is known for their experimental and innovative music, their lyrics often focus on social and emotional themes. Incubus is also famous for his energetic stage performances and has toured many times around the world.

Incubus' achievements include Grammy nominations, winning several music awards and selling millions of albums. The group has a prominent place in the alternative rock scene with their unique and expressive sound that combines various musical styles.
